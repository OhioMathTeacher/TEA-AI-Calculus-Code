## 7) Missed opportunities (elaborated)

1. **Deeper Exploration of Historical Context:** The AI could have encouraged the student to explore more about Brook Taylor's contributions and the historical significance of his work, fostering a deeper connection to the material.
   
2. **Encouraging Multiple Scenarios:** The AI could have prompted the student to consider multiple real-world scenarios before settling on one, enhancing their ability to apply Taylor polynomials in diverse contexts.

3. **Reflection on Learning Process:** The AI could have asked the student to reflect on their learning process at various stages, promoting metacognitive skills and deeper understanding.

4. **Connecting to Modern Applications:** The AI could have linked the discussion of Taylor polynomials to modern computational methods, providing a broader perspective on their relevance today.

5. **Encouraging Peer Discussion:** Suggesting that the student discuss their ideas with peers could have enriched their understanding through collaborative learning.
